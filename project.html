HOW TO MAKE A MINI-VACUUM CLEANER

  • Materials

    • A recycle bottle
    • A pair of scissors
    • A knife
    • A motor
    • A glue gun
    • A pencil
    • A ruler
    • An empty can
 

STEP 1 : CUT THE BOTTLE

Start by measuring about 2 inches from the bottom of the bottle and cut round the circumference of the bottle to seperate it into two pieces. Now place the shorter piece on a table and make aseries of holes in the bottom with your knife. TIP : The more you make,the better air will circulate and the stronger your vacuum will be.

STEP 2 : PLACE THE MOTOR IN THE BOTTLE

Add some hot glue to the back of the motor and place it on the bottom and in the middle of the bottle, make sure the motor sits properly in the bottle. Let out the power wire through the vent holes.

 

STEP 3 : MAKE THE FAN

Open up the can into a plane sheet. Place the bottle part with the motor face on the sheet and trace the outline of the bottle with your pencil. Cut along the outline to make a circular sheet. Divide the circle into eight equal parts. Cut along the outline and stop about half way to the center, fold each section in the same direction to produce a fan with eight blades. Glue the fan to the motor shaft facing the holes you created earlier.

 

STEP 4 : RE-ASSEMBLE THE BOTTLE

Tape the two halves of the bottle together with a duct tape. Make a good seal, but don't over do it since the tape has to be removed to empty the vacuum. After re-assembling the bottle add a nozzle to the mouth of the bottle and tape it.

 

STEP 5 : CONNECT THE MOTOR TO A POWER SOURCE

Finaly, connect the motor wires to a source of electricity (i.e a battery) and start vacuuming.
